# Typical French Green Salad

A French family might serve poulet rôti with:
- Lettuce (or mixed leaves)
- A few tomatoes (optional)
- Vinaigrette

Nothing fancy—often just lettuce dressed at the last minute.

## Vinaigrette

Vinaigrette is the classic French salad dressing. At its simplest, it's just:

- 3 parts oil
- 1 part vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper

A French person might joke that a salad is merely a vehicle for eating good vinaigrette. It's so fundamental that many households make it from scratch several times a week rather than buying bottled dressing.
